About Me

HI I am ladd mauigoa. Been through a lot lately. I have a degree in biotechnology. I was going to go to dental school,but I lost passion for it. One thing I have always liked was technology. So I dabbled a little bit in html and css. Found out I really liked it. So I went and took a dev bootcamp. I absolutely loved it! It was hard but I loved what I was learning and I loved the people I was around. I now find myself not having the time to keep learning! There is so much to do!


I love being able to code and make a web app from scratch with the mean stack. I use a javascript full stack. I started with javascript as a beginning language. Now I want to move onto more of a low level programming language soon after I have learned a little more of testing.
